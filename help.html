Booking a meeting

How do I book a meeting?

Pick one of the three meeting types (15, 30, or 60 minutes), then choose a highlighted date on the calendar. The time slots for that day appear on the right. Select a time, enter your details, and click Schedule Event. A confirmation will be sent to your email.

Why are most days greyed out?

Two reasons. First, bookings open 3 days in advance, so the soonest date is always a few days out. Second, availability is limited — only days with free slots are highlighted in blue. Check back tomorrow if nothing fits.

Can I book on a weekend?

Not currently. Saturdays and Sundays are blocked by default.

How far in advance can I book?

Up to 60 days. Anything beyond is not offered.

Can I reschedule or cancel?

Yes. Use the "Reschedule" or "Cancel" links on the confirmation page, or find them in your calendar invite email.

Do I need an account to book?

No. Attendees can book without signing up. Only hosts need an account.

Account & sign-in

How do I create an account?

Visit the Sign up page and enter your name, email, and password. We'll send a verification email; confirm it to activate the account.

I forgot my password

Go to Sign in and click "Forgot password". We'll email you a reset link. Links expire after 1 hour.

How do I connect Google Calendar?

After signing in, open Settings → Integrations → Google Calendar, and authorise access. You can disconnect at any time from the same page.

How do I change my timezone?

We detect it from your browser automatically. To override, go to Settings → Preferences → Timezone.

How do I delete my account?

Settings → Account → Delete account. This permanently removes your data within 30 days.

Troubleshooting

I didn't receive a confirmation email

First, check your spam or junk folder. If it isn't there, wait a few minutes — email delivery can be delayed. Still nothing? Try adding noreply@meet.example to your contacts and book again.

The Google Meet link doesn't work

Google Meet links are generated when you confirm a booking. Make sure you're signed in to the same Google account that was invited. If the link is broken, reply to the confirmation email and we'll regenerate it.

Time slots show in the wrong timezone

We use your browser's timezone. If it's wrong, check your operating system's timezone setting and reload the page.

Which browsers are supported?

Latest versions of Chrome, Firefox, Safari, and Edge. We don't support Internet Explorer.

The calendar shows no available days at all

Availability refreshes daily at local midnight. If the host is at capacity for the week, check back tomorrow — new slots open up then.
